How to Pay Fees – UISTO

The Management of the University of Innovation, Science and Technology (UISTO) operates a fully online payment system. All payments must be made exclusively through the official UISTO portal.

These guidelines explain how to pay the three (3) approved fee heads:

  • Post-UTME Application Fee – Compulsory for all applicants
  • Supplementary Application Fee – Compulsory for all applicants
  • Acceptance Fee – Compulsory for admitted candidates only
⚠ IMPORTANT NOTICE

Do NOT pay any fee into any individual or personal bank account. The University shall not be held liable for payments made outside the approved UISTO payment platform.

1. Post-UTME Application Fee

Category Selection

  • (A) Already filled Post-UTME form and paid the fee
    👉 Click here to pay the Supplementary Application Fee
  • (B) Filled Post-UTME form but not paid the fee
    👉 Click here to pay the Post-UTME Application Fee
  • (C) Yet to fill Post-UTME form and pay the fee

Steps for Category (C)

  1. Visit the Application portal
  2. Complete the personal information form
  3. Submit to generate your payment invoice
  4. Click PAY NOW on the invoice page
  5. Pay using Debit/ATM card or Bank transfer
  6. You will be redirected back to the portal after payment
  7. Upload your O’Level results
  8. Submit the application
  9. Print the Application Summary Page

2. Supplementary Application Fee

All applicants who have submitted their Post-UTME application must pay the Supplementary Application Fee.

  1. Visit: Supplementary Portal
  2. Enter your JAMB Registration Number
  3. Click PAY NOW
  4. Generate payment invoice
  5. Pay using Debit/ATM card or Bank transfer
  6. Download and print your receipt
Important Note:

If you submitted your Post-UTME form without paying the Post-UTME fee, the system will first generate that invoice. Complete it, then repeat the process for the Supplementary fee.

3. Acceptance Fee

Payment of the Acceptance Fee confirms a candidate’s admission.

  1. Log in to the UISTO student portal
  2. Go to the Acceptance Fee section
  3. Click PAY NOW
  4. Pay via Debit/ATM card or Bank transfer
  5. Download and print the receipt
  6. Keep the receipt for registration and clearance

General Payment Advisory
