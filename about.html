About Our UISTO

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision, and Objectives

The University of Innovation, Science and Technology, Omuma (UISTO) was established by the Imo State of Nigeria Law No. 6 of 2025, signed into law by His Excellency, Distinguished Senator Hope Uzodimma, on August 26, 2025. This foundational legislation reflects the state government’s commitment to strengthening innovation-driven education, advancing scientific capabilities, and creating new opportunities for Nigerian youth.

Objectives

  • Providing training, research, and development in applied arts, sciences, technology, management, and commerce.
  • Offering full-time and part-time courses leading to diplomas, certificates, and other qualifications.
  • Organizing conferences, seminars, and public lectures to disseminate knowledge.
  • Preparing students for examinations set by the Institution or other examining bodies.
  • Developing techniques to meet the needs of Imo State and Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be a world-class university of innovation that drives sustainable development through cutting-edge research, transformative teaching, entrepreneurship, and the application of science, technology, and design for societal advancement.

Our Mission

The mission of the University of Innovation, Science and Technology, Omuma (UISTO) is to provide holistic, forward-looking education that integrates science, technology, management, and creative disciplines to produce highly skilled, innovative, and entrepreneurial graduates.

The University is committed to fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and research excellence, while cultivating partnerships with industry, government, and communities to address national and global challenges. UISTO upholds the values of excellence, integrity, inclusiveness, and sustainability in all its academic and professional endeavours.

Institutional Mandates

Training & Research

Advancing applied arts, sciences, technology, management, and commerce through structured learning and innovation.

Academic Certification

Delivering both full-time and part-time programs that lead to accredited diplomas and certificates.

Societal Impact

Innovating solutions to meet the industrial and societal needs of Imo State and Nigeria at large.

Knowledge Dissemination

Hosting conferences, seminars, and public lectures to share insights and academic advancements.
